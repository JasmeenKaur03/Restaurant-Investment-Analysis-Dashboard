## 🍽️ Restaurant Investment Analysis – Power BI Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing customer behavior and restaurant data to guide smart investment decisions.

# 1. Business Problem
Restaurant investors in San Luis Potosi lacked data-driven 
insights on where to invest, which cuisines attract the most 
customers, and what customer profiles dominate the market — 
leading to risky, uninformed investment decisions.

# 2. Goal of the Dashboard
To build a visual analytics report that helps investors answer:
- Who are the customers and what are their preferences?
- Which cuisines are most popular and profitable?
- Where is the best location to invest in the city?

# 3. Key Visuals
- **Customer Age Groupings** – KPI cards showing 80% of customers are 18-25 yrs, 13% are 26-45 yrs, 7% are 46+
- **Smoking & Alcohol Preferences** – Stacked bar charts segmented by age group
- **Budget Distribution** – Most customers fall in the medium budget range
- **Most Popular Cuisine by Age Group** – Heatmap showing cuisine preferences across age groups
- **No. of Customers per Restaurant by Cuisine Type** – Bubble chart comparing restaurant count vs customer footfall
- **Geo Map – San Luis Potosi** – Ratio of customers to restaurants by location to identify investment hotspots

# 4. Key Insights
- 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 **80% of customers are aged 18-25** — a young, price-sensitive demographic
- 🚭 Most customers **prefer smoke-free spaces**
- 🍹 **Drinking is common** — alcohol-friendly restaurants have an edge
- 🌮 **Mexican & Bar cuisines** have the most restaurants but low average customer count
- 🍔 **Fast Food, Seafood & Breweries** attract significantly more customers per restaurant
- 📍 **San Luis Potosi central areas** have the highest customer density — ideal for new investments

# 5. Business Impact
This dashboard empowers investors to:
- ✅ Target the **18-25 age group** with affordable, smoke-free dining concepts
- ✅ Invest in **Fast Food or Seafood** over Mexican/Bar for better customer-to-restaurant ratio
- ✅ Choose **central San Luis Potosi** for maximum customer footfall
- ✅ Avoid oversaturated cuisine types and focus on **underserved but high-demand** categories

# 6. Data Source
Source: [Maven Analytics](https://www.mavenanalytics.io/)
Data on restaurants and customer profiles in San Luis Potosi,
including cuisine types, customer demographics, smoking/alcohol
preferences, budget ranges, and geolocation data.

# 7. Tech Stack
- 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Data visualization platform
- 🗂️ **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
- 🧠 **DAX** – Calculated measures and dynamic visuals
- 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Table relationships for cross-filtering
- 🗺️ **Map Visual** – Bing Maps integration for geo analysis
- 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for development, `.png` for preview
